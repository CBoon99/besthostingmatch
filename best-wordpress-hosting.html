Managed WordPress hosting handles updates, backups, caching, and security for you — so you can focus on the site, not the server. Here's how the leading hosts score.

Managed vs shared for WordPress: Shared hosting runs WordPress fine for small sites on a budget. Managed WordPress costs more but adds automatic updates, expert WP support, staging, and serious caching. If WordPress is your business, managed pays for itself.
