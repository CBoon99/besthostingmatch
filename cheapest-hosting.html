How to actually get the cheapest hosting

  • Buy the longest term. The intro price usually only applies if you pay 2–4 years upfront. It delays the renewal jump too.
  • Check the renewal, always. A $1.99 host that renews at $11.99 costs more over three years than a $2.99 host that renews at $4.95.
  • Skip the add-ons at checkout. Site backups, "SEO tools", and premium support upsells inflate the bill. Most you don't need.
  • Use the money-back guarantee. DreamHost gives 97 days, InMotion 90 — plenty of time to test before committing.
