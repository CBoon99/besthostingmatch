The takeaway

  • Always check the renewal price before you buy — it's often 3–8× the intro rate.
  • A higher headline price can be cheaper over three years. SiteGround vs the cheapest shared hosts is the classic example.
  • Managed WordPress hosts (Kinsta, WP Engine, Cloudways) usually don't play the intro-discount game — what you see is what you pay.
  • Lock in the longest term you're comfortable with to delay the renewal jump.
