Privacy & legal

The short version: we collect no personal data, run no tracking cookies, and capture no email addresses. This page explains exactly what does and doesn't happen when you visit.

Plain-English summary: Best Hosting Match is a static website. There are no accounts, no logins, no forms, no newsletter, and no advertising trackers. We don't build a profile of you. The few technical details below are there for full transparency.

1. Who we are

Best Hosting Match ("we", "the site") is an independent web hosting comparison site operated by Carl Boon as an individual. There is no registered company entity. The site is published from the United Kingdom and hosted on Netlify's global infrastructure.

2. What data we collect

Directly: none. We have no contact forms, no sign-up, no newsletter, no comments, and no accounts. We never ask you for your name, email, or any personal information.

Automatically: like virtually every website, our host (Netlify) processes standard server request logs — including IP addresses and browser user-agent strings — to deliver pages and protect against abuse. We do not have access to a database of these logs, do not use them to identify you, and do not combine them with anything else.

3. Cookies and local storage

We set no tracking cookies, which is why you won't see a cookie-consent banner — there's nothing non-essential to consent to. The site stores a single, purely functional value in your browser's local storage called hosting-theme, which remembers whether you chose light or dark mode. It never leaves your device, contains no personal data, and you can clear it anytime via your browser settings.

That's the complete list. We run no analytics (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, nothing), no advertising pixels, and no social-media trackers. If we ever add privacy-respecting analytics, this page will be updated first.

5. External links

When you click a "Visit site" button, you leave Best Hosting Match and go directly to the hosting provider's own website. We're not responsible for their content or privacy practices — read their policies once you're there. Outbound links currently point to official provider homepages; we earn nothing from them (see disclosure below).

6. Affiliate & advertising disclosure

Current status: no affiliate links are active. The site earns no commission and accepts no payment for placement. If affiliate partnerships are added in future, they will be clearly disclosed here and on our ethics page, marked with appropriate rel attributes, and — critically — they will never influence a score or ranking.

7. Editorial disclaimer

Our scores are editorial opinions based on published specifications, SLA documentation, and public benchmark data — not first-person lab tests. We work to keep data accurate and date every update, but hosting prices and features change frequently. Always verify current pricing and terms on the provider's own site before purchasing. Nothing here is professional, legal, or financial advice.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR and similar laws, you have rights over personal data held about you. Because we hold no personal data and operate no database of visitors, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete. Server logs are controlled by Netlify under their privacy policy.

9. Changes to this policy

If the site's data practices change — for example if analytics or affiliate links are introduced — this page will be updated and re-dated before the change goes live.
